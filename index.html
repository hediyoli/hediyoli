I am Yuanheng Li and born in August 6th,1990. I am a singer and a businessman. I interest in the Rock and Classical music, and I also work on these kinds of music. I also do some works about international trade, international culture exchange, and the techniques of music industry.

Audio – Dylan Li Falling Down

EDUCATION

From 2008 to 2010, I studied electronic music composition in Essen, Germany. The college was named Folkwang. I also learned some program of modern music and classical music. From 2010 to 2012, I studied in Beijing Defense, Science, and technology University in China. I got my bachelor’s degree there, and my major was international business management. From 2012-2014, I learned business management in San Francisco State University. After 2015, I began to learn vocal performance as my master’s degree in McNally Smith Music college. My singing area is the Rock music. Therefore, I have two majors as a student, one is business, the other one is music.



WORK

I have trying to do some jobs. From 2008, I began to open my own store in Beijing, and our store sold the clothing of designers’ brands, and we also have online store. From 2010 to 2012, I worked in Beijing Mei Shi Jia Yin science development limited company. This company is focus on building the audio engineering for music hall, large gymnasium, or the multi-function theater. At that time, I learned many information of music techniques. In the fall of 2012, I worked in Master international trade company, and I worked in the marketing department. It was my part-time job. When I studied in San Francisco, I worked in De Beckon artist association. I organized two musical competitions in the San Francisco area and brought around 100 voice and piano students to the event from China.



MUSIC INFLUENCE AND STYLE

My music style is influenced by Muse, Radiohead, Chopin, Liszt, Rachmaninoff, Paganini, Tchaikovsky, Beethoven, and Bach. I want to create a new musical mixture, and I want to mix Chinese traditional music, Rock music, and Classical music together in the future.



PERFORMANCE AND ACTING

I have some performing experience from 2006 to 2016. There are piano competitions, vocal competitions, music festivals, and some recitals of school. I also acted a TV-Show. In the summer of 2015, I acted a Chinese TV-Show, which is named Young Doctor. I am a singer and a businessman, I will continue to develop my career on culture industry. Music is the thing, which can fulfill my spirit, and the business can support my true life.
